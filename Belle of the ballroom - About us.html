Belle of the Ballroom is your once stop shop for all of your ballroom dance needs, from shoes to practice wear to custom made costumes, we have everything you could ever need. Located in the heart of the GTA we are easy to reach both by car and public transportation. With hardwood flooring and great lighting you can feel the way your shoes will feel on the dance floor and see the way your costume will shine in the light. Drop by our store today and let us turn you into the Belle of the Ballroom.

Practice makes perfect. And what makes your practice perfect? The perfect practice wear. We have a large selection of practice wear for any occasion and style. With Chrissane and Crystal Clover fabrics, you can rest assured that you'll be getting your money's worth. Drop by our store and transform your practices forever.

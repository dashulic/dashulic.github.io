Ending deforestation is about YOU. Here's what you can do to save your planet.

1. Plant a tree:



Now this one might seem super simple and not very helpful, what can planting one tree accomplish, right? Wrong. There are 7 billion people on this Earth. If one persone plants one tree, there won't be a diference in the garnd scheme of things. However, if 10% of the people on this planet each planted one tree there would be 70 million more trees and that would make a siggnificant difference. Planting trees won't stop deforestation, but it can help counter the effects of it our planet.

2. Recycle:



In Grade 1 we are taught the 3 Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. If we follow this one simple rule we can lower the amount of deforestation that occurs. By recycling and using products made from recycled materials we are eliminating the need for new trees to be cut down. By doing this we are also sending a mesage to big companies and corporations and telling them that we do not agree with what they are doigna dn the fact that they are killing our planet.

3. Raise your voice:


The world isn't going to change itself, if you want to see a change in the world you need to make it happen. If you want deforestation to end you need to tell large companies and corporations that you arenot okay with them killing our planet. Protest against their policies and boycott their products, if no one buys their products they'll be forced to stop making them, or start making them in an eco-friendly way.
